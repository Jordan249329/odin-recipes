Vanilla Ice Cream

Description

Everyone's favorite dessert

Ingredients

  • 2 1/4 cup milk
  • 1 cup heavy whipping cream
  • 3/4 hite sugar
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Cook milk, cream, and sugar until sugar has dissolved
  2. Add vanilla extract to mixture
  3. Chill overnight
  4. Pour mix into an ice cream maker and churn
  5. Serve Immediateky or ripen in freezer before serving
