Raspberry Lemonade

Description

A quick and easy drink to make during the summer time.

Ingredients

  • 1 can frozen raspberry-lemonade concentrate
  • 3 cups water
  • 3/4 teaspoon lime juice
  • 1 can lemon-lime flavored carbonated beverage
  • 1 cup crushed ice
  • 1 cup fresh raspberries

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, combine raspberry-lemonade concentrate, water, and lime juice
  2. Stir mix, adding lemon-lime carbonated beverage and crushed ice while stirring
  3. Serve and garnish each glass with a fresh raspberry
