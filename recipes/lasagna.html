Lasagna

Description

An instant classic to enjoy at any family dinner

Ingredients

  • Meat(Italian Sausage and lean ground beef)
  • Onion
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 1 can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cans tomato sauce
  • 2 cans tomato sauce
  • 2 tablespoons of white sugar
  • Lasagna noodles
  • 1 Egg
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Ricotaa cheese

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook noodles
  3. Make ricotta mixture
  4. Layer lasagna as follows:
    1. Meat sauce
    2. Noodles
    3. Ricotta mixture
    4. Mozzarella slices
    5. Meat sauce
    6. Parmesan cheese
    7. Repeat layers, then top with parmesan cheese
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving
